Density is an intrinsic property of matter that indicates how much mass a material has per unit of volume. ρ = m/V
It's expressed in units such as kg/m³ or g/cm³.
If we use weight (W = mg) instead of mass in the relationship, we obtain the specific weight: γ = W/V, and γ = ρg

Intrinsic

Something that is inherent or essential to the nature of a thing, coming from within, and not dependent on external factors.
The word "intrinsic" comes from the Latin word "intrinsecus," meaning "situated on the inside".
